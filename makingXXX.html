Making Masala Chai

So you wanna make Masala Chai!

Get the ingredients and lets go....


Ingredients

7 green Cardamom Capsule

1 thumb of ginger

1 small piece of cinnamon

2 teaspoon of crush curl of Assam tea (PG)

2 teaspoon brown sugar

450-ml milk (min 3,5% fat)

150-ml tap water

Crush the cardamom capsules with your fingers and cut the ginger into small pieces.

Transfer all spices to a small saucepan, add water and milk and bring to a boil.

Let it simmer for 18 minutes and add the sugar in the end.

Pour the tea through a sieve then strain it into a warmed teapot or directly into teacups.
